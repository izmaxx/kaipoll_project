What Are We Voting For?

Basically, there's a logistical limit to the number of dishes we can offer at any given time. Part of it is storage/prep space and the other aspect is our ability to handle the rushes.

To make the best of it, we rotate our specials. Every week, we get requests on bringing back different specials from the past or for new things. This is your chance to help us figure out what to do next...power to the people!
